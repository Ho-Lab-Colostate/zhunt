About Z-hunt

Z-hunt was developed to calculate the probability of Z-DNA formation based on free energy of a given DNA sequence. Energies are based on in vitro experiments to determine nucleotide to nucleotide interactions in either a syn or anti conformation.

Z-mhunt is an updated version of Z-hunt developed in 2020. Z-mhunt calculates exactly the same as Z-hunt while also incorporating methylation sites. Methylation energies are also based on in vitro experiments and are reflected in the overall z-score
